## Sections present (known surface, 2026-04-29)

[No dedicated brand guidelines page. Key obligations inferred from
consumer terms + general knowledge.]

1. Name / Logo Prohibition Without Permission
   — "You may not, without our prior written permission, use our name,
      logos, or other trademarks in connection with products or services
      other than the Services, or in any other way that implies our
      affiliation, endorsement, or sponsorship."

2. Wordmark / Trademark Coverage
   — "Anthropic", "Claude", "Claude Code", "claude.ai", any Anthropic-
     registered logos, the Claude wordmark all fall under this restriction.

3. No Endorsement / Affiliation Implication
   — Third-party products may NOT imply Anthropic endorsement, affiliation,
     or sponsorship unless a written partner-program agreement is in place.

4. Correct Product-Name Capitalisation (general knowledge, not confirmed
   from a live page but consistent with Anthropic's published style)
   — "Claude" (capital C, rest lowercase)
   — "Anthropic" (capital A)
   — "Claude Code" (two capitalised words)
   — "claude.ai" (lowercase for domain, no implication that the product
     offers claude.ai access)

5. No Modification of Wordmarks
   — Logos, wordmarks, and product names may not be altered, combined
     with other marks, or used in ways that could cause brand confusion.

6. Contact for Permission
   — marketing@anthropic.com for brand / trademark use requests.
   — Note: as of 2026, no public Anthropic partner program is known to
     exist for third-party tools. Permission requests are assessed case-
     by-case and may be denied without explanation.
